Kanye West

Kanye Omari West
June 8, 1977 (age 40)
Chicago, Illinois, US.

Kanye West is a polarizing figure to some but he has solidified himself as a visionary of the age. From pursuing his dreams by becoming a rapper on Roc-a-fella Records, he released College Dropout in 2004 to critical acclaim and commercial success. The accolades for his albums has continued for over 15 years with ventures in fashion, film and art escalating to new heights.
